How To Use the Platform

1. Begin using the website by clicking on the "View Map" button on navigation bar

2. You will be redirected to a page which maps all the different Farmers Markets in Prince George's County. Along with the map, there will be table on the left side of the page which will display the names of the market along with the website associated with the market.

3. Explore the map to your liking and find a market that interests you. Click on a dropped pin on the map to get detailed information about the market. This will open up a sidebar on the map with the location and the phone number of the market

4. Once you find a market of your liking you can enter in the address of the market in any naviation platform of your liking and visit the market.

5. Additionally we are working to add inforamtion on the different products that are avaliable at each market. This helps to notify the user which market they should visit to find the product they are looking for, in turn cutting down time spent looking for products.
